Rupali Khose

Born On June 1976 in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
She was a disciple of Sri Amma Bhagavan. For the last 22 years, she has served Oneness Organisation. Helped thousands of youth, families and also served Tribals for there welfare. Rupali ji has created Oneness Dance Therapy to heal people physically and mentally through music and dance. She is a speaker, counselor and has conducted various spiritual programs for people's inner transformation, external growth and success. Apart from this, she has also performed many pujas, abhishekas and fire rituals with Vedic chanting. She has been guiding people as an astrologer and Vastu expert.
In a very special and unique way, Rupali ji is getting divine guidance and is able to help people. She is able to communion with Divine. She is the Founder and Head of Aadya Divine Foundation started on 17 August 2023.

Aadya Foundation

People of every age group on the planet need proper guidance, clarity about purpose of life, happy and healthy relationships, physical and mental health and communication with God. Aadya Foundation is helping everyone in these different ways to lead an amazing life. It has two main sections which will be conducted directly by Rupali ji which are Divine Chamber and Counseling Sessions.
